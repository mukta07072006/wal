About Us

Our journey from a vision to a thriving community resource

Founded in 2003 by a group of young visionaries

Under the leadership of Erfan Ullah, World Amity Library began as a small collection of donated books in a rented space. Today, we've grown into a beloved community institution serving thousands of readers annually.

Our mission is to make knowledge accessible to all, regardless of economic background, and to foster a culture of reading and lifelong learning.

Our Goals

What we strive to achieve in our community

Increase reading rates by providing free access to books and educational materials for all age groups.

Create programs that bring people together through book clubs, workshops, and cultural events.

Our History

Key milestones in our journey

2003

Foundation

World Amity Library was founded by Erfan Ullah and a group of young visionaries with just 500 donated books.

2005

First Permanent Location

Moved to our first dedicated building, allowing us to expand our collection and services.

2010

Digital Expansion

Launched our digital library platform, making resources available online.

2015

10,000 Books Milestone

Reached our goal of 10,000 books in the collection, serving over 5,000 members.

2023

20th Anniversary

Celebrated 20 years of service with over 30,000 books and 15,000 active members.
